I'm a digital product designer with 8+ years of experience across Korea and the Netherlands. I partner with early-stage and sustainability-focused brands to clarify their positioning and translate it into cohesive digital systems.

My work goes beyond interfaces. I help define what a brand stands for and how that clarity scales across products and touchpoints. Combining research-driven insight, psychology-informed thinking, and system-level design, I turn complex challenges into structured, human-centred, and strategically aligned experiences.

I design not just for screens, but for long-term positioning and impact.
